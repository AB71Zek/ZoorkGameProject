Commands in ZOOrk Game:
----------------------
go <direction> - Move in the specified direction (go north, go east, go south, go west, go down, go up).
look - Display if there are items available in the room.
look <object> - Display a message indicating object details or that the object isn't found.
take/get <item> - Pick up an item from the current room.
drop <item> - Drop an item from your inventory into the current room.
inventory/i - Show the contents of your inventory.
quit - Exit the game.
